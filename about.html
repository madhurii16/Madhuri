About Us

Learn more about our mission, goals, and how we contribute to a sustainable future.

Our Mission

We aim to revolutionize the way e-waste is managed by creating an efficient and eco-friendly system that benefits users, businesses, and the environment. Our platform connects individuals with local collection spots, provides pricing estimates for e-waste, and promotes responsible recycling practices.

Key Features

  • Locate nearby e-waste collection spots.
  • Accurate pricing calculator for e-waste.
  • Real-time tracking of collection and recycling progress.
  • Dedicated portal for administrators and users.

Why Choose Us?

Our platform is designed with simplicity, efficiency, and sustainability in mind. We believe in making e-waste management accessible to everyone and promoting a circular economy where waste is transformed into resources.
